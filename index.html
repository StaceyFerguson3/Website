Your Ideal Birth Plan Can Be A Reality

Are you interested in having a doula with you during any or all stages of pregnancy, birthing and post-partum? A doula can have a positive impact as a support for you during one of the most important times in your life.

As your doula, I am there to take care of the non-medical aspects of your experience. My role is to provide support for your whole family circle and ensure your loved ones are included in your care.

Many of our traditional ceremonies and birth practices are coming back. I am here to support how you want your culture and ceremony incorporated. If you're uncertain, talk to your elders if at all possible. Both women and men had/have roles throughout the circle so be sure to ask everyone!

Can you afford a doula?

With this resource, you can afford a doula! Funding is available for Indigenous families through the Doulas for Aboriginal Family Grant Program. The goal of the program is to increase healthy birth outcomes for Indigenous families by removing the cost barrier to accessing doula services. They also provide a listing of available doulas who are part of the program.
